Who We Are

Born and Raised in Paradise

ZanteLocals was created by a group of friends who grew up on this beautiful island. We saw too many tourists missing out on the real Zakynthos - the hidden taverns with the best fish, the secret beaches only locals know, and the authentic experiences that make this island magical.

We're not a travel company. We're not paid by restaurants or bars. We're just locals who want to share our home with you the way we experience it - genuine, unfiltered, and unforgettable.
